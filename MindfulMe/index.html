What MindfulMe does

MindfulMe is a calm, private space to log how you feel, write about your day, and reflect with gentle AI prompts.

  • Mood tracking to capture how you feel and when.
  • Guided journaling to explore your thoughts and experiences.
  • AI reflections that help you notice patterns and possible coping strategies.
  • Supportive resources with exercises and crisis information when you need extra support.

Not a replacement for psychologists

MindfulMe is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, or provide emergency care.

It is designed as a self-help and reflection companion that complements, not substitutes, support from a licensed mental health professional.

If you are in crisis, feeling unsafe, or worried about harming yourself or others, please contact local emergency services or a crisis helpline immediately.

For individuals

Use MindfulMe to track moods, journal freely, and talk to the AI therapist between appointments. Over time, you can see patterns in triggers, routines, and coping strategies that support your mental health journey.

  • Notice what lifts you up or wears you down across days and weeks.
  • Capture thoughts and moments you may want to share later in therapy.
  • Arrive at sessions with concrete examples instead of trying to remember everything.

For psychologists

With your patient's consent, MindfulMe can make it easier to understand what happens between sessions and how life events influence their emotional state.

  • Review mood trends and significant notes over time.
  • See high-level themes from journals and AI reflections.
  • Use this context to focus time together on what matters most.
